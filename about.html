Our Story

About Divine’s Touch

Who we are:

From a small workshop to a lifestyle destination

Divine’s Touch was founded in Johannesburg, Turffontein in 2018 by an entrepreneur; Divine Mussa, a former mobile technician who noticed a growing gap in the local market for reliable , affordable, and trustworthy repair services.


What began as a small one- man cell phone repair workshop operating in a neglected building in Turffontein quickly earned a loyal following for its fast turnaround times, honest diagnosis and genuine spare parts. In 2025, Divine and his team took a bold step of launching a modern hair salon and barbershop under the same roof. The beauty division was designed to complement the tech side. The business soon evolved into a full retail service hub, adding trendy clothing and accessories to cater for the everyday needs of its community.
